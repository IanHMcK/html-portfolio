My Favourite Films by Ian

My top 3 films of all time.

The Blues Brothers

An all time classic starring Dan Ackroyd and John Belushi.
Ideal film if you're a fan of Blues music and classic car chases

Interstellar

A very interesting sci-fi film...if you can ignore the fact you couldn't actually travel through a black hole.

6 Go Underground

What I'd class as a Sunday afternoon film, one to put on and not have to think about it, plenty of action.
